Why Timely?

Timely helps you focus. In a better way. We know that you are busy. Want to concentrate on something for a while. Just use Timely to count time down.

Want to take a break for a book? Set the time to given amount of time and start reading - we will handle the time counting for you.

Or maybe it's time to catch a nap? No worries - our team also is doing the same. Set the Timely and sleep well.
